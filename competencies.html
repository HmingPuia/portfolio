Railway App

We built this project as part of a four-person team for our EvolveU project 3. The country of Nigeria has recently been developing its rail and metro network. Unfortunately, train schedules are only available as PDF documents with no digital solution. Rail-Way attempts to resolve this issue by providing a dynamic rail schedule presented as either the next departing train for a route or a route's weekly schedule. Also, the application provides travelers with a basic trip planner.
